My Familyyy

I come from a loving family of eight, and I’m the fifth out of six siblings. My family means the world to me. they’ve always been my biggest source of motivation, the reason I strive to do my best and never give up.

My father, who serves as our village head, is someone I deeply admire, a man of wisdom, integrity, and hard work, respected by many. My mother, on the other hand, is the heart of our home. She’s a warm and patient homemaker who fills our days with love, care, and comfort.

Growing up, my parents taught me the true meaning of respect, kindness, and education. No matter how far i go or how busy life gets, my family will always be my safe place, the people who keep me grounded and remind me of who i am.
